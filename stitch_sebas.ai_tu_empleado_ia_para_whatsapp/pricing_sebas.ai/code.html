Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an existing WhatsApp API account?

No, we handle the WhatsApp API integration for you. You simply need a phone number to dedicate to the service during setup.

What counts as a "message"?

A message is counted for every discrete interaction sent or received by the AI assistant. Automated system notifications and human handoff messages do not consume your AI quota.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can adjust your plan at any time from your billing dashboard. Changes are prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.
